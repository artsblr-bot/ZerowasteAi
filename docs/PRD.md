# ZeroWaste AI — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Phase 0: Foundation

---

## 1. Mission

To eliminate food waste at the source by providing every food business with an intelligent decision-making system that predicts demand, prevents overproduction, and connects surplus with those in need — making food waste economically unprofitable and socially unacceptable.

## 2. Vision

A world where zero food is wasted because every meal is prepared with purpose, every surplus finds a home, and every food business operates with perfect intelligence about what to cook, how much to prepare, and where surplus should go.

## 3. Problem Definition

### 3.1 The Problem
- **1.3 billion tons** of food is wasted globally every year (FAO)
- **$1 trillion** in economic losses annually
- **8%** of global greenhouse gas emissions come from food waste
- **828 million** people go hungry daily — yet we waste enough food to feed them all
- Restaurants are among the largest contributors, wasting **15-20%** of food purchased

### 3.2 Why Existing Solutions Fail
| Problem | Current Solutions | Why They Fail |
|---------|------------------|---------------|
| Data entry burden | Manual waste logging apps | Restaurants don't have time |
| Reactive approach | Donation-only apps | Waste already happened |
| No demand prediction | Static inventory sheets | Doesn't adapt to daily changes |
| No financial incentive | Charity-focused platforms | Restaurants lose money donating |
| Fragmented ecosystem | Multiple disconnected tools | No unified intelligence |

### 3.3 Root Cause
The core problem is not that restaurants don't care about waste — it's that they lack **the intelligence to predict demand accurately**. Every day, chefs guess how much to cook. When they guess wrong, waste happens. ZeroWaste AI replaces guessing with prediction.

## 8. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Low restaurant adoption | Medium | High | Free tier, zero data entry onboarding |
| Poor prediction accuracy early on | High | Medium | Cold start with segment averages, wide confidence intervals |
| Integration complexity (POS systems) | Medium | Medium | Build adapter pattern, prioritize top 5 POS systems |
| Food safety liability | Low | Critical | Food Safety AI + temperature monitoring + disclaimers |
| NGO unreliability (no-shows) | Medium | Medium | Reputation scoring, backup NGO matching |
| Regulatory uncertainty | Medium | High | Legal counsel, compliance-by-design, local partnerships |
| Funding / runway | Medium | High | Lean team, focus on paid pilots, government grants |

## 11. Core Product Value Proposition

> **For food businesses:**
> ZeroWaste AI tells you exactly how much to cook, when you'll have surplus, and where it should go — before you start cooking. You save money, reduce waste, and generate measurable social and environmental impact, all with zero extra effort.

> **For NGOs:**
> ZeroWaste AI delivers verified, safe, and predictable surplus food directly to your door, with real-time tracking, temperature monitoring, and impact reporting.

> **For volunteers and drivers:**
> ZeroWaste AI gives you a simple mobile experience to pick up and deliver food with optimized routes, QR verification, and real-time impact tracking.

> **For governments and corporations:**
> ZeroWaste AI provides auditable, verifiable data on food waste reduction, CO₂ avoidance, and social impact for ESG, CSR, and policy reporting.
